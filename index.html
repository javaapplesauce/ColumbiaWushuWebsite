About Us

Founded in 2010, Columbia Wushu has grown to become the largest collegiate Chinese martial arts club in the NYC area. We welcome a large family of practitioners from all backgrounds, ages, levels of experience, and walks of life.

Whether you are hoping to train with us long-term or only visiting the Big Apple for a few days, please feel free to drop by our practices!

Book a Performance

We perform both at on-campus events and across the city! You can find past recordings on our YouTube channel. If you are looking to book a performance with us, please email us with the date and location of the show, as well as time length and space size information. Let us know if you have any questions!
